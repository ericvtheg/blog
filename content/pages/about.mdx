## Who Am I

Eric is a 27 year old software engineer with 6 years of experience. His focus currently is in designing and creating distributed systems.

He works as a web developer for [iFIT](https://www.ifit.com/), where he designs and implements data intensive applications.

He has a passion for helping people through software. In his spare time, he works on ways to empower talented music producers. 

## Projects

- [MAKID](https://makidapp.com/) an Ableton Live Project manager desktop application with 1000+ MAU. Built to help music producers manage their tracks.
- [betatestsongs.com](https://betatestsongs.com) a web app that enables EDM producers to anonymously share and review unreleased music
